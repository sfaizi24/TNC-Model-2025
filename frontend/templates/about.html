How the model works

  • I automate scrapes from five projection sites and align everything with Sleeper's roster data, even the multi-position edge cases.
  • I layer in injuries and byes to rebuild each lineup before anyone remembers to set theirs.
  • I capture variance from all five feeds, treat player outcomes as log-normal, and fire 50,000 Monte Carlo runs per team.
  • I translate the simulated distributions into the odds you see on the board.
